## 1. Summary

- The App stores **game progress and preferences on your device** (offline save data).
- The App shows **ads** through **Google AdMob** and may use **Google Play Billing** for optional in-app purchases. Those services are operated by **Google** under their own terms and privacy policies.
- We do **not** operate our own account system inside the App for sign-in; we do **not** intentionally collect your name or email through the App itself.

---

## 2. Information the App processes on your device

### 2.1 Local game data (stored by the App)

The App saves data locally on your device (for example under the app’s private storage), including items such as:

- Puzzle progress, daily puzzle selections, and related game state  
- Settings (for example appearance / display preferences, optional gameplay options)  
- Aggregated statistics (for example completion counts, streaks, timers)  
- Counts related to optional boosts (for example hints, quick pencil charges, second chances)

This data is used **only to run the App** (resume games, show stats, apply settings). It is **not** sent to our own servers by the App code we ship; it remains on the device unless the operating system backs it up according to your device settings.

**Removal:** Uninstalling the App typically deletes this local data, subject to your device manufacturer and OS behavior.
